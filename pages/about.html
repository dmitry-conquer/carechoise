ABOUT US

Our Story:

From Local Roots
to Nationwide Impact

At CareChoice we believe that family caregivers are the backbone of our communities. You dedicate your time and energy to providing the best care for your loved ones, often juggling multiple responsibilities. We’re here to support you every step of the way.

As our reputation for compassionate, participant-centered care grew, we expanded throughout Pennsylvania—building lasting community ties and refining our approach to meet the diverse needs of every family we served. Over the years, thousands of families have placed their trust in CareChoice, relying on us to provide the highest standard of care and support.

Today, CareChoice proudly supports families in multiple states. With over two decades of experience, we continue to deliver exceptional care while honoring each individual’s independence, dignity, choice and right to remain at home.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to empower family caregivers by providing reliable support and resources. We understand the challenges you face, from navigating Medicaid to finding fair compensation for your invaluable work. At CareChoice, we prioritize your needs and ensure that you have everything you require to succeed.

What We Do

We specialize in helping family caregivers enroll in Medicaid’s program while offering better pay and benefits. Our experienced team is committed to ensuring you receive the support you deserve, without cutting corners. We pay on time, every time, and our dedicated staff is always available to assist you.
