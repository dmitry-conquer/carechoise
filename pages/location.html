Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to empower family caregivers by providing reliable support and resources. We understand the challenges you face, from navigating Medicaid to finding fair compensation for your invaluable work. At CareChoice, we prioritize your needs and ensure that you have everything you require to succeed.

What We Do

We specialize in helping family caregivers enroll in Medicaid’s program while offering better pay and benefits. Our experienced team is committed to ensuring you receive the support you deserve, without cutting corners. We pay on time, every time, and our dedicated staff is always available to assist you.

FAQs

Yes. Pennsylvania’s Community HealthChoices (CHC) program allows family members to get paid for providing care to loved ones at home. Instead of relying on a stranger, your loved one can choose you or another trusted family member to be their caregiver.

CareChoice helps Pennsylvania families apply, handle all paperwork, and make sure caregivers are paid on time with fair wages, benefits, and recognition.

How the program works in Pennsylvania

  • Who qualifies: Adults who are eligible for Medicaid and need help with daily activities like bathing, dressing, cooking, or mobility.
  • Who can be the caregiver: A family member or close friend chosen by the participant — often an adult child, sibling, or spouse.
  • How caregivers are paid: Competitive hourly wages, with overtime pay and benefits available in many cases.
  • Support included: Training, ongoing resources, and advocacy from CareChoice.
